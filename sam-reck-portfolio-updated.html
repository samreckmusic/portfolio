About

I’m a music producer specializing in production, mixing, and mastering for artists across genres. I also release my own music to showcase a unique blend of sonic styles and technical skill.

Services

  • Songwriting: Unique lyrics, stand-out melodies, harmony with emotional weight, and addictive arrangements tailored to each artist’s unique sound.
  • Mixing: Delivering industry-ready mixes with sonic clarity, space, and balance.
  • Mastering: Quality control for streaming, radio, and physical formats with the right amount of perceived loudness and tonal balance for your genre.
  • Artist Development: Giving independent artists the tools for long-term growth. Access to real-world industry experience with a focus on sound identity and reaching an audience through a coherent release strategy.
